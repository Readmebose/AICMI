Data is Everywhere

More than 2.7 zettabytes of data exist in todayâ€™s digital universe and that is projected to grow to 180 zettabytes in 2025. Data science, also known as data-driven science, is an interdisciplinary field about scientific methods, processes, and systems to extract knowledge or insights from data in various forms, either structured or unstructured. Data Science is a field that encompasses anything related to data acquisition, data cleansing & transformation, preparation, and model building and analysis.

Everything is Data

All the data around you be it tangible or intangible has some value to it that can be extracted though science.

Make Sense of it

Data science enables you to make sense of the data and make the most of it.

Analytics/ Data Science industry in India

Analytics/ Data Science industry in India There are some studies that believe that the Analytics/ Data Science industry in India is at a phase where IT was some 10-15 years ago and thus a boom can be expected in analytics outsourcing to India. Hence, Data Science has a great scope in India.

Nasscom pegs revenues from data science and AI (IT and non-IT industries) in the country at $16 billion by 2025, providing jobs to 1.50 lakh professionals. The expertise in Data Science is not be high in India (except some pockets), and therefore there is a strong reason to form a Society of Data Science.
