Membership of AICMI

Membership is open to data scientists, scientists, students, academicians, and others interested in science and the data science profession. Membership is available under three categories namely Professional, Student and Institution.

Benefits Of Membership

  • Free access to quarterly newsletter containing cutting edge updates in this domain.

  • Discounted rate on workshops and Certification programmes on disruptive technologies, conducted by experts.

  • Discounted registration fee to the flagship International Conference on Data Management, Analytics and Innovation (ICDMAI).

  • Access to global list of mentors, especially for students to jointly undertake writing of papers, developing projects and open source packages.

  • Opportunity to deliver workshop/training in collaboration with Association for Intelligent Computing and Mathematical Innovation(AICMI).

  • Opportunity for networking with the S4DS members

  • Data Management, Curation, Preservation

Establish a Student Chapter

Student Chapter can be started at any institution with minimum 12 Student members and 03 Professional members.

Benefits Of a Student Chapter

  • Association with Technical Professional Body will help in receiving recognition by Accreditation bodies.

  • Establish center of excellence on many disruptive technologies.

  • Conduct Certification Programmes on disruptive technologies.

  • Resource and promotional support for Workshop, Lectures, Seminars, Conferences etc.

  • Organize Faculty Development Programmes and Technical Training Programmes.

  • Opportunity to host or become partner in organizing the International Conference on Data Management, Analytics and Innovation (ICDMAI), a flagship conference of AICMI.

  • Opportunity to organize activities to develop entrepreneurial skills, leadership skills, soft skills and other skill development activities.

We are a non-profit professional association to create a collaborative platform for bringing together technical experts across Industry.
